# Moran Plumbing Services — Website Rebuild Project Brief

## Executive Summary

Rebuild the website for Moran Plumbing Services, a family-owned Phoenix-area plumbing company. The current Wix site has broken pages, missing SEO optimization, and no blog. The new site will be built with Next.js, optimized for local SEO/AEO/GEO, and designed to capture leads across their service areas.

## Content Tone Guidelines

### DO
- Use "we" and "our" (family feel)
- Mention community and relationships
- Be direct and honest about services
- Include specific Arizona references
- Use action-oriented language
- Keep it conversational but professional

### DON'T
- Be overly corporate or stiff
- Use excessive jargon
- Make unrealistic promises
- Forget the human element
- Ignore local context

### Sample Voice
> "At Moran Plumbing Services, we're not just here to fix your pipes—we're here to build a relationship. When you call us, you're calling family. Whether it's a midnight emergency or a new home build, no job is too big or too small. We've been proudly serving the Phoenix community with integrity, and we'd be honored to help you too."
